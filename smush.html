Smush

———designed at Concordia University

Smush is a website that I designed and developed as an experiment into how multisensorial interactions can engage with users to evoke emotional responses and encourage prolonged participation. The unconventional nature of this site counters the typical nature of the web in order to introduce a variety of interactive elements that might have broader applications elsewhere online.
