Type to save a life

———designed at Canadian Blood Services

On Tuesday, Feb. 4, 2020 Canadian Blood Services and volunteer student recruitment teams hosted blood typing events in Universities across Canada. Events in 26 cities across the country resulted in 3,660 students learning their blood type.

Our team created the assets necessary to promote the campaign as well as run the events day of. My contribution included designing wall mounted ads, digital banners, newspaper ads, flyers, posters, swag and selfie sign props.
