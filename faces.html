Editorial Design

FACES Magazine

———designed for FACES Magazine

Faces Magazine is a monthly Lifestyle publication that showcases Ottawa’s people, places and events. Each issue is complementary – and features local events, photos from around the city as well as a variety of fashion, health and fitness articles.

In 2019 I had the opportunity to design some spreads for their Fall and Winter publications. I was given carte blanche to create layouts for interviews as well as wellness, lifestyle and fashion articles.
