Synacor Push App

———designed with Synacor

At Synacor Ottawa, I was tasked with creating the redesign of an internal push notification tool. The increased efficiency and usability of the new design allowed Synacor’s development team to operate their applications for multiple major clients.
I worked on designing location settings for the Start App, one of Synacor’s major projects for AT&T. I took these screen states through the full design process from sketches, to wireframes and user experience flow charts up to the final designs.
