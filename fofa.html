Publication Design

USE Catalogue

———designed for Fofa Gallery

Material Trace
is the 2019 Undergraduate Student Exhibition Catalogue. Working with a team of designers (Lindsey Carter, Andira Hernandez, Carolina Ovalle, and Gavin Park) we executed an archive of selected artworks shown at the Fofa Gallery from 2017 to 2018. Through the catalogue’s concept, our team came to understand the reader as a collector with an interest in art objects. This catalogue is meant to capture the accumulative processes of the artists and, in turn, ask the viewer to take part in this methodical practice of collection.
