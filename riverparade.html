Ride the River

———designed for Ottawa Riverkeeper

Ottawa Riverkeeper is a river conservation organization in the Ottawa region. In the summer of 2017 they launched a new event; Ride the River Parade. I led the design projects for this event, producing a new brand identity, website, and all promotional materials.
